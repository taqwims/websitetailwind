About FillsUp

Our Story

Fashion Market was born from a passion for style and a vision to make quality fashion accessible to everyone. What started as a small dream has now blossomed into a thriving fashion empire, serving customers across Indonesia and beyond.

Our Journey

2022 - The Beginning

Started as a small studio in Bandung with a dream to revolutionize fashion retail.

2023 - Going Digital

Launched our e-commerce platform, bringing fashion to customers nationwide.

2023 - Expansion

Opened 10 new stores across major campus in Indonesia.

2024 - Today

A leading fashion retailer with over 50 campus and a thriving online presence.
